Project Completion
December 15, 2025

Downtown Office Complex Grand Opening

BuildPro successfully completed the Downtown Office Complex, a state-of-the-art 50,000 square foot commercial building. The project was delivered on schedule and under budget, exceeding client expectations with innovative sustainable design features and modern amenities.

The building has earned LEED Gold certification and features advanced energy-efficient systems, reducing operational costs by 40% compared to traditional buildings.

Award Recognition
November 28, 2025

BuildPro Wins Regional Excellence Award

BuildPro has been recognized with the Regional Construction Excellence Award for outstanding achievement in quality craftsmanship and innovation. The award highlights our commitment to delivering world-class construction services and maintaining the highest safety standards.

This is our third consecutive year receiving this prestigious honor from the National Builders Association.

New Project Launch
November 10, 2025

Groundbreaking for Riverside Residential Community

BuildPro broke ground on the Riverside Residential Community, an expansive 200-unit mixed-use development featuring modern apartments, townhomes, and retail spaces. This project represents a $85 million investment in sustainable urban development.

The community will feature green spaces, smart home technology, and pedestrian-friendly design. Expected completion is scheduled for Q4 2027.
