About BuildPro

Building Dreams, Creating Communities

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction solutions that transform visions into reality, while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and customer satisfaction. We are committed to building structures that stand the test of time and create value for our clients and communities.

Our Story

BuildPro Construction was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate construction professionals with a shared vision: to revolutionize the construction industry with integrity, innovation, and excellence. What started as a small team of five dedicated builders has grown into a leading construction company with a portfolio of over 500 completed projects.

Our founder, Marcus Thompson, began his career as a construction worker in his early twenties, learning every aspect of the trade from the ground up. Frustrated by the lack of quality and professionalism in the industry, Marcus decided to start his own company with a simple promise: deliver superior quality at fair prices, on schedule, every single time.

Over the past two decades, BuildPro has earned a stellar reputation for reliability, craftsmanship, and customer service. We've transformed neighborhoods, revitalized commercial districts, and provided families with the homes of their dreams.
